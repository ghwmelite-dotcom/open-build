- The stakes: *"The Pra, the Ankobra, the Birim — rivers Ghana drinks from — are running brown. Turbidity has hit ~14,000 — water plants are built for about 2,000."*
- Frame it: *"This isn't about naming anyone. It's about making the damage impossible to ignore."*
- Ask the chat: **"Is a river near you affected? Just the place — no names."**
